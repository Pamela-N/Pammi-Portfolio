About Me




Junior Front-End Developer






Skills and Passions


LANGUAGES

I am fluent in English, Zulu ,Sesotho and Setswana whilst proficient in Afrikaans.

SPORTS

Growing up with brothers has given me a love of sports, having been part of the Netball, Cricket and Soccer teams. I reached the district level with Netball and coached the Junior teams in High School.

PUBLIC SPEAKING

It’s a passion of mine to stand in front of a crowd and speak whether it is at an Eisteddfod evening, Public Speaking, or simply presenting my work to my class and lecturers.

CREATIVE

As a creative, my top tier is writing which includes poetry and just writing essays. I enjoy taking photos of people and editing them to look even better, I love coming up with ideas to create content and to solve problems.



My Future Goals




My goal is to be associated with a company where I can utilize my skills and gain further experience while enhancing the company’s productivity, helping them to meet their targets and representing them in a good light.


To continue learning more about technology and it’s vast range of careers that one can grow into.


One thing that I dream about, is that one day I get to teach what I have learned along the way and give back to young women - especially those who don’t know where to start. I hope to be a mentor to those coming after me.
