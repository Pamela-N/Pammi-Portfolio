About Me





I am a young aspiring creative coder who is constantly looking for new ways to solve problems. Being raised in different backgrounds has given me a different outlook in life and has taught me to appreciate the smallest wins too.


I am a witty hard worker with a positive outlook on life. I am interested in how technology is changing the world and excited about the role I am going to play in it.



I am an ADA graduate!





A journey that began in 2018, the day I registered online with Harambee landed me on the doorstep of my dreams.
Ever since I was 15, I knew I wanted to be in the IT field but had not discovered coding as a path I could follow

Out of many brilliant and bright women I was part of the 12 that was chosen to start the programme and I knew that this was meant for me.
